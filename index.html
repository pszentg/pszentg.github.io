Robotic Engineer

I designed a complete, easy to upscale robotic infrastructure for the purpose of replacing human interaction during the testing POS terminals. I selected the motors, designed the 3D models for the joints, eventually moving the parts into a metallic box-shaped frame with ball-bearing rails.
